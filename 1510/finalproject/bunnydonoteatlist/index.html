Bunny Do Not Eat List

The following food should be avoided as they are toxic to your bunny:

Avocados Chocolate Fruit seeds/pits Raw onions, leeks, garlic Meat, eggs, dairy Broad beans and kidney beans Rhubarb Iceberg lettuce Mushrooms House plants Processed foods (bread, pasta, cookies, crackers, chips, etc.) Raw potatoes
